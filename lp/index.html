Welcome to CoinMill

CoinMill is a multi-service solution for the projects to source funds as well as to promote their tokens within the wider audience on Coinex Smart Chain and reward token holders and liquidity providers through flexible stake2farm solutions.

CoinMill v.1 will feature CoinPad (an IDO platform) and CoinFarm (farming solution).

It will later include other elements like the lottery, prediction market, and NFT-farming to both improve user experience and create additional deflationary flows for the platform native token.

farming solution

CoinFarm

CoinFarm allows projects to popularize their tokens, increase liquidity and reward their token holders and liquidity providers by offering their tokens in exchange for staking their liquidity tokens of pools on OneSwap or any other CSC tokens

IDE PLAtform

CoinPad

CoinPad provides projects with the necessary tools to run an IDO. This includes advertising, user KYC process, further assistance with listing on CSC DEX projects like OneSwap and CoinEx itself. The Launchpad team executes a due diligence process to check the team, the project behind, mitigate possible scam risks, etc.
